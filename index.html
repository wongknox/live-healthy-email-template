Tips for better
gut health

TIP #1

Eat a high-fiber diet

A variety of plant foods, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and legumes, provides the diverse fibers needed to feed many different beneficial gut bacteria.

TIP #2

Try fermented foods

Foods like yogurt with live and active cultures, kefir, sauerkraut, and kimchi introduce beneficial microorganisms directly into your digestive system.

TIP #3

Manage stress levels

Incorporate stress-reduction techniques such as regular exercise, meditation, or spending time outdoors to help keep your gut calm and healthy.
